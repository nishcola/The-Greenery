5 Vegan Pantry Staples for Easy Weeknight Meals

Published on 3/4/2025 by The Greenery

Eating delicious, healthy vegan meals during a busy week doesn't have to be complicated! Having a well-stocked pantry is key. Here are five versatile staples we always recommend keeping on hand at The Greenery (and that you can easily stock at home):

1. Legumes (Beans & Lentils)

Canned or dried, legumes are protein powerhouses. Think chickpeas for hummus or crispy roasting, black beans for tacos and bowls, kidney beans for chili, and lentils (red, green, brown) for soups, stews, salads, or hearty fillings like our Shepherd's Pie. They're affordable, nutritious, and incredibly versatile.

2. Whole Grains

From comforting oats for breakfast to the base of our popular Buddha Bowls, whole grains provide sustained energy and fiber. Keep quinoa, brown rice, farro, or whole wheat pasta on hand. They form the foundation of countless satisfying meals.

3. Canned Tomatoes

Diced, crushed, or puréed – canned tomatoes are flavor bombs essential for pasta sauces, soups, stews, curries, and more. They offer year-round tomato flavor when fresh ones aren't in season.

4. Nuts & Seeds (and their Butters!)

Cashews for creamy sauces, walnuts for salads, chia and flax seeds for binding or adding omega-3s, tahini (sesame seed paste) for dressings – nuts and seeds add healthy fats, texture, and flavor. Nut butters (peanut, almond) are great for snacks, sauces (like our Spicy Peanut Noodles!), or smoothies.

5. Spices & Nutritional Yeast

A good spice rack is crucial for flavor! Essentials include cumin, paprika (smoked is great!), garlic powder, onion powder, oregano, and chili powder. And don't forget nutritional yeast ('nooch')! This deactivated yeast adds a cheesy, savory, umami depth to sauces, tofu scrambles, and pasta dishes – it's a vegan kitchen superstar.

With these staples in your pantry, you're always just a few steps away from a delicious and satisfying plant-based meal!
