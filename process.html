Local & Seasonal Sourcing

Whenever possible, we source our vibrant produce from local farms right here in the Pennsylvania area, like Threefold Farm and Brook Meadow Farm. This reduces transportation emissions ("food miles"), supports our local agricultural economy, and guarantees the freshest, most flavorful ingredients arrive in our kitchen. Our menu evolves with the seasons to reflect nature's best offerings.

Waste Reduction & Composting

Minimizing waste is a top priority. We practice careful inventory management ("root-to-stem" cooking where feasible) and partner with CompostNow to compost all unavoidable food scraps – turning potential waste into valuable soil amendment. We utilize reusable containers extensively in our kitchen operations and train staff on proper sorting to maximize recycling efforts.

Eco-Friendly Packaging

For our takeout customers, we've chosen packaging with the planet in mind. Our containers are made from plant-based, compostable materials (like bagasse), and our cutlery is either compostable or reusable upon request for large orders. We actively seek suppliers who share our commitment to minimal and sustainable packaging solutions.

Energy & Water Conservation

We strive for efficiency in our resource use. Our kitchen features Energy Star rated appliances where possible, and we've implemented LED lighting throughout the restaurant. Staff are trained on best practices for water conservation during prep and cleaning. We regularly review our utility usage to identify areas for further improvement.

Looking Ahead: Our Future Goals

  • Achieve 80% waste diversion rate by 2026.
  • Introduce a reusable container program for regular takeaway customers by Q1/2027.
  • Source 50% of our electricity from renewable sources via PPL by late 2025.
  • Host quarterly workshops on sustainable cooking/plant-based eating.
  • Continuously research and implement innovative water-saving techniques in the kitchen.

We believe sustainability is an ongoing journey. If you have suggestions or questions about our practices, please contact us!
