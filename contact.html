Frequently Asked Questions

Yes, we accept reservations for parties of 4 or more. Please call us during business hours at (123) 456-7890 to book your table. For smaller parties or walk-ins, we operate on a first-come, first-served basis, but feel free to call ahead to check wait times.

There is metered street parking available on Plantain Place and surrounding streets (check signs for hours/restrictions). There is also a public parking garage located two blocks east on Veggie Ave, near the corner of Bean St.

Your health and safety are important to us! Please inform your server of any allergies or dietary restrictions immediately upon ordering. While our kitchen is 100% vegan, we do handle common allergens like nuts, soy, gluten, and sesame. We have procedures to minimize cross-contamination, but cannot guarantee zero risk for severe allergies. Check our menu for item-specific icons (GF, NF, SF options) and use the "Special Instructions" field when ordering online. Don't hesitate to ask staff for clarification.
