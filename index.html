The Greenery

Welcome to The Greenery, the largest Vegan-friendly restaurant in Central PA. Here, you'll find everything to meet your dietary needs, from Buddha Bowls filled with juicy vegetables to chocolate mousse that is sure to leave a sweet taste in your mouth! We do all of this while ensuring our committment to sustainability.
