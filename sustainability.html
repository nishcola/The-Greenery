Sustainability Practices

Our commitment to the environment.

Farm-to-Table

We source our ingredients from local farms to reduce food miles and support sustainable agriculture. By doing this, our local community is supported and we get ingredients for a much cheaper price as compared to other more expensive producers.

Eco-Friendly Packaging

All our takeout packaging is compostable and biodegradable, helping reduce waste. With less waste, there is more opportunity for our environment to thrive and less room for animals that could potentially get harmed.

Energy Efficiency

Our restaurant uses energy-efficient appliances to reduce our carbon footprint. By doing so, we facilitate the growth of more new plants in our local farm area to slow down carbon emissions.

Water Conservation

We are practicing water-saving techniques to minimize our impact on local water resources. This gives animals nearby more water to thrive on, and us people also get an increased amount of drinking water while still getting fed properly.
