Welcome to Green Table

Fresh, locally-sourced vegan & vegetarian dishes served with sustainability first.

Our Philosophy

At Green Table, we believe in providing delicious, plant-based meals created with fresh, locally-sourced ingredients. Our farm-to-table approach ensures that every dish is crafted with sustainability and health in mind.

Sustainability

We prioritize eco-friendly practices in every aspect of our business. From sourcing ingredients from nearby farms to using compostable packaging, Green Table is committed to making a positive impact on the environment.

Farm-to-Table

All our produce comes from local farms, reducing food miles and supporting sustainable agriculture.

Compostable Packaging

All packaging and takeout materials are 100% compostable, minimizing waste.

Water Conservation

We are aiming to minimize water use in both our kitchen and dining area.
